## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account information:** name (chosen name), phone number, date of birth, gender, and role (dater or wingperson)
- **Dating profile:** bio, city, interests, and relationship preferences
- **Photos:** profile photos you upload or that your wingperson suggests on your behalf

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Device information:** device type, operating system version, and push notification token
- **Usage data:** app interactions such as swipe decisions (like/pass)

### Information From Third Parties

- **Apple Sign In:** if you authenticate via Apple, we receive a user identifier and optionally your email address, subject to Apple's privacy policy
- **Phone verification:** when you verify your phone number, Supabase Auth processes the OTP delivery

We use the information we collect to:

- Send transactional SMS messages (see Section 3)
- Send push notifications (new matches, messages, wingperson invites, and suggestions)
- Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse

## 3. SMS Messaging

We send SMS messages for two purposes only:

### Wing Invite (A2P / Application-to-Person)

When a dater invites a friend to become their wingperson, we send a one-time SMS invitation to the friend's phone number. The recipient must actively accept the invitation within the app before any additional SMS messages are sent. Message frequency: one message per invite.

### OTP / Phone Verification (Transactional)

When you create an account or log in, we send a one-time passcode (OTP) to your phone number to verify your identity. Message frequency: one message per sign-in or verification event.

**Message and data rates may apply.**

To opt out of wing invite SMS messages, reply **STOP** to any message. Reply **HELP** for assistance.

**Your phone number and SMS opt-in data will not be shared with or sold to third parties for marketing purposes.**
